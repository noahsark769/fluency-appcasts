Fluency 0.0.14

Thank you for beta testing Fluency!

Improvements and Fixes

  • Fluency can favorite and unfavorite pages and show your favorite pages in the sidebar
  • Recent spaces are now shown in Add Space window
  • Added new controls to show when spaces were last updated, and the ability to refresh them
  • Spaces now refresh once a day to ensure your pages are up to date
  • Fixed an issue where nested bullet points would sometimes have an incorrect indentation level
